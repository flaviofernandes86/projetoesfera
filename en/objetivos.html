Objectives

Main objective

Identify the authenticity and possible origin of Brazilian crop circles.

Specific objectives

  • Build a national catalog of all crop circles found in Brazil.
  • Analyze and interpret electromagnetic waves emitted by these signs using advanced equipment.
  • Investigate similarities and systemic coincidences in the phenomenon.
  • Compare Brazilian crop circles with international ones through exchange between countries studying the phenomenon.
  • Perform laboratory analyses to verify the authenticity of the phenomenon.
  • Capture 3D images of possible electromagnetic fields generated by Brazilian crop circles.
  • Analisar filmagens em alta velocidade e fotografias em infravermelho e ultravioleta.
  • Confirm or refute the hypothetical electromagnetic energy cone at crop-circle centers.
